Lasagna

Description

Prepare to make the best lasagna. This will be the "ultimate" lasagna, no other lasagna shall surpass the lasagna you are about to make. It will be the King of lasagnas. Find out why it will be the King of lasagnas below...

It will be the "King" of lasagnas because I said it will. You can trust and believe I am correct because I am a King and you are not. Don't tell me what to do.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package of oven ready lasagna noodles
  • 2 lbs. of sweet italian sausage (browned)
  • 2 lbs. of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 32 oz. part skim ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 lb. brown mushrooms (sliced, optional)
  • 3 cloves garlic (crushed and minced, optional)
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Spray oil in the bottom of a 7-inch cake pan or ramekin. Place some noodies in the bottom (break them to size). Spoon on marinara sauce on top of the noodles. Sprinkle the meatball slices or if you use the sausage, pre-cook it with the mushrooms and garlic and sprinkle them on.
  2. Scoop the ricotta cheese on top of the meat and finish the layer by covering it with mozzarella cheese. Follow this for a second layer. Finish the top with the remaining cheese.
  3. Pour the water in the bottom of the Instant Pot, place the tricet in the bottom, and set the lasagna on top of the trivet. Place the lid on the Instant Pot and select manual, pressure, high for 30 minutes. Let the pot finish the cycle and bleed off naturally. Let it sit for 10 minutes or more before serving.
